1. Application Overview

KoraKick is a mobile application designed to connect football players across Morocco. The app allows users to discover and join pickup games, book football facilities, create and manage teams, and participate in tournaments. Our platform facilitates the organization of matches by providing tools for scheduling, team formation, and communication between players.
